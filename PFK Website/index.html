About PFK Academy

Empowering the Next
Generation of Creators

PFK Academy is Egypt's premier coding and STEM education institute for kids aged 6 to 17. We offer live, online courses in web development, game design, robotics, and artificial intelligence.

Since 2016, we've helped thousands of students develop the skills to innovate and create value in a technology-driven world.

STEM Accredited Program

Our Mission

To make coding accessible, fun, and impactful for every child, empowering them to become creators of technology.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of innovative thinkers who use technology to solve real-world problems and shape the future.
